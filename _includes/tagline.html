A once-in-a-generation opportunity

The COVID-19 pandemic forced federal agencies to dramatically expand telework. While the move to distributed work first seemed like an experiment, it has since revealed itself to be good business: federal employees continued to successfully accomplish their agencies’ missions in 2020–2021, challenging preconceived notions of how, when, and where federal work can be performed.

GSA launched Workplace 2030 to learn from this once-in-a-generation phenomenon, and to fundamentally update federal stakeholders’ understanding of work processes.
